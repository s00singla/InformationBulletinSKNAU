	\begin{SectionBox}{What is Ragging?}
		Ragging is a criminal offence and lowers standards of education. Any act resulting in:
		\begin{itemize}[noitemsep,nolistsep]
			\item Mental / Physical
			\item Sexual abuse
			\item Verbal abuse
			\item Indecent behavior
			\item Criminal intimidation/ Wrongful restraint
			\item Undermining human dignity
			\item Financial exploitation/ Extortion
			\item Use of force
		\end{itemize}
	\end{SectionBox}	
	
	\begin{SectionBox}{A Student indulging in ragging can be:}
		\begin{itemize}[noitemsep,nolistsep]
			\item Expelled from the institution
			\item Banned from the mess/ hostel
			\item His / her scholarship can be withdrawn
			\item Debarred from examinations
			\item Denied admission to any institution
			\item Prosecuted for criminal action
		\end{itemize}
	\end{SectionBox}
